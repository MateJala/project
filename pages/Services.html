How We Damage Nature
  • Littering: Trash harms wildlife and takes centuries to decompose.
  • Deforestation: Cutting trees destroys habitats and worsens climate change.
  • Water Pollution: Chemicals and waste harm water and aquatic life.
  • Air Pollution: Smoke and harmful gases contribute to global warming.
  • Overuse of Resources: Wasting water, electricity, and minerals harms ecosystems.
How to Protect Nature
  • Recycle & Reuse: Sort recyclables and reuse items like bottles and bags.
  • Save Water: Turn off taps and fix leaks.
  • Plant Trees: Join tree-planting drives or grow a plant at home.
  • Use Less Energy: Switch off unused lights and use energy-efficient devices.
  • Avoid Plastic: Opt for reusable items instead of single-use plastics.
