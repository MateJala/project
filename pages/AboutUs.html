Introduction:
  • to "Protect the Earth – It Is Our Home." Our mission is to educate and inspire the next generation to take action in preserving nature and creating a sustainable future.
  • This website was created to simplify the complexities of environmental protection and make them accessible to school students.
Goals:
  • Raise awareness about the importance of protecting our planet.
  • Provide practical tools and knowledge to empower young minds.
  • Showcase efforts by organizations and individuals working tirelessly for the environment.
Our Values:
  • Sustainability: Advocating for a balance between human activities and nature.
  • Education: Making information about environmental protection engaging and understandable.
  • Action: Encouraging simple, impactful steps to make a difference.
Team:
  • Our team consists of educators, environmentalists, and creative contributors who are passionate about helping students understand the importance of a healthy planet.
Foundations
World Wildlife Fund (WWF)
  • Focus: Conservation of wildlife and natural habitats.
  • Known for: Global campaigns and initiatives like Earth Hour.
Greenpeace
  • Focus: Advocating for a greener, more peaceful world through direct action.
  • Known for: Campaigns on climate change, deforestation, and ocean protection.
The Nature Conservancy (TNC)
  • Focus: Protecting land, water, and oceans worldwide.
  • Known for: Scientific approaches to conservation and large-scale projects.
Rainforest Alliance
  • Focus: Conserving biodiversity and ensuring sustainable livelihoods.
  • Known for: Certification programs for sustainable agriculture and forestry.
Ocean Conservancy
  • Focus: Protecting the oceans from threats like pollution and overfishing.
  • Known for: International Coastal Cleanup campaigns.
Wildlife Conservation Society (WCS)
  • Focus: Protecting wildlife and natural habitats globally.
  • Known for: Conservation programs in over 60 countries.
