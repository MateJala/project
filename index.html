Discover how you can help protect nature

Learn how we can all protect the earth together.
Caring for nature is essential because our planet provides the resources we need to survive—clean air, water, and food. By protecting nature, we help ensure a healthy environment for ourselves and future generations. This website will guide students in understanding the small but powerful actions they can take to make a positive impact, from reducing waste to supporting environmental organizations, empowering them to become responsible caretakers of the Earth.
Quick Facts
  1. Every year, we lose around 10 million hectares of forest—an area roughly the size of Iceland. Forests are crucial for clean air, wildlife, and combating climate change.
  2. Over 8 million tons of plastic end up in the ocean every year, harming marine life and polluting our waters.
  3. One tree can absorb about 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year, helping to reduce the effects of climate change.
  4. Coral reefs cover less than 0.1% of the ocean’s surface but support 25% of marine life, making them vital to ocean health.
  5. The Earth's temperature has increased by approximately 1.2°C since the late 1800s, with major consequences for ecosystems and weather patterns.
